BPA-CSIRO Workshops

bpa-csiro-workshops

Welcome to the BPA-CSIRO Workshops Project on GitHub. This page provides an overview of the workshops and training modules that are maintained by the group. Each workshop repositories and its corresponding training modules are described in detail below.

Getting Started

To get started on how to reuse existing BTP workshops or developing new workshops, please visit the BTP Workshop Template page on GitHub. To know how the BTP images and instances are created and launched, please visit the BTP Orchestration page on GitHub.

Introduction to Next Generation Sequencing

This is a practical 3 day hands-on course designed for bench biologists, PhD students or early career postdoctoral researchers with no or basic bioinformatics experience who are planning or currently using NGS approaches in their research area. It will include a half-day introduction to the command line interface.

Training Modules
Data Quality
Read Alignment
ChIP-Seq
RNA-Seq
de novo Genome Assembly

Cancer Genomics

This is a practical 3 day hands-on course designed for cancer biologists, wet lab scientists and clinical researchers, which aims to provide an introduction to cancer genomics analytical pipelines for single nucleotide variation (SNV), copy number variation (CNV) and structural variation (SV).

Training Modules
Data Quality
Read Alignment
Single Nucleotica Variant Call and Annotation
Copy Number Variation
Variants Visualization
Structural Variant Analysis
Assessing Somatic Mutational Signatures

De novo Assembly

The de novo assembly workshop will provide an introduction to de novo assembly, with hands on exercises followed by in-depth analysis of the key steps in the process. It will cover aspects such as quality control and pre-processing of datasets, generation and evaluation of first pass assemblies, assembly improvement and scaffolding. Practical exercises will be performed on small-scale real datasets, and will include best practices.

Training Modules
Introduction to CLI
Data Quality
Bacterial Assembly
Eukaryotic Assembly
Scaffolding
