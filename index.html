BPA-CSIRO Workshops

Advancing bioinformatics expertise among Australian ‘omics researchers.

Welcome to the BPA-CSIRO workshops project on GitHub. This serves as a landing page for the various training workshops organised and maintained by BPA-CSIRO. An overview of the training workshops and included training modules are described below. For the latest upcoming BPA-CSIRO workshops, please visit the Bioplatforms Australia training page.

Introduction to Next Generation Sequencing Hands-on Workshop

This is a practical 3 day hands-on course designed for bench biologists, PhD students or early career postdoctoral researchers with no or basic bioinformatics experience who are planning or currently using NGS approaches in their research area. It will include a half-day introduction to the command line interface. The training modules included in this workshop are listed below.

Training Modules
Data Quality
Read Alignment
ChIP-Seq
RNA-Seq
de novo Genome Assembly

Getting Started

The Introduction to Next Generation Sequencing Hands-on Workshop image can be launched on the NeCTAR Research Cloud and Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud platforms. For the NeCTAR Research Cloud, the latest image is: (image name: BTP-2016-01-25, image id: 9c7fd8ac-5cfe-4067-aae3-82e53a49def3). A detailed guide on launching the image on the NeCTAR Research Cloud is available here. On AWS, the image (AMI) is available on the following regions: (ap-southeast-2: ami-be597ddd, au-west-1: ami-d3a31ea0). A detailed guide for launching the image on AWS is also available here. Stand-alone images are also available for VirtualBox and VMWare. The stand-alone images are downloadable from here.
